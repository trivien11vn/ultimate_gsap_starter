Elastic Easing

Elastic easing in GSAP creates a bouncy, spring-like motion that overshoots and oscillates before settling into place.

It mimics the effect of pulling and releasing a rubber band, making animations feel dynamic and playful.

Variants like elastic.in, elastic.out, and elastic.inOut control how and when the bounce occurs.
